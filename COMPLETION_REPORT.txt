USAGE INSTRUCTIONS

1. BROWSING POSTS
   - Open the 📰 Feed tab
   - Posts are sorted by relevance (highest first)
   - Use Advanced Filters to narrow results
   - Click "🔗 Open Link" to view on Reddit

2. SAVING POSTS
   - Click "✓ Engage" on any post
   - Post is saved and disappears from Feed
   - Post appears in 💾 Saved Posts tab
   - Active Posts count decreases

3. MANAGING SAVED POSTS
   - Open the 💾 Saved Posts tab
   - View all saved posts
   - Click "✖ Unsave" to remove a post
   - Post returns to Feed
   - Use "Clear All Saved" to reset everything

4. CHANGING THEME
   - Click "☀️ Light" or "🌙 Dark" button (top-right)
   - Theme changes instantly
   - All colors adapt automatically
   - Theme persists during session

5. FILTERING POSTS
   - Click "⚙️ Advanced Filters" to expand
   - Set Minimum Relevance Score
   - Select Time Window (24h, 3d, 7d, 30d)
   - Choose specific Subreddits
   - Search by Keywords
   - Filters apply in real-time
