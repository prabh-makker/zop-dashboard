TECHNICAL SUMMARY
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

Algorithm Flow:
1. Fetch all posts (network or cache)
2. Calculate relevance score for each
3. Filter out engaged/saved posts
4. Sort by relevance score (descending)
5. Shuffle the entire list randomly ← KEY CHANGE
6. Return top 6 from shuffled list

Why This Works:
• Randomization on entire list (not just top 6)
• Maintains quality (posts still sorted by relevance first)
• Provides variety (random selection)
• No performance impact (shuffle is O(n))
