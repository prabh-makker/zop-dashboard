## 🎯 Daily Workflows

### Want to Search for Posts?
1. Go to 📰 Feed tab
2. Type keywords in 🔍 Search box
3. See top 10 ranked results
4. Click Engage or Open Link

### Want to Save a Post?
1. Find post in Feed
2. Click ✓ Engage button
3. Post moves to Saved Posts tab
4. View anytime in 💾 Saved Posts

### Want to Switch Theme?
1. Click button in top-right corner
2. Shows ☀️ Light or 🌙 Dark
3. Colors change instantly
4. No page reload needed

### Want to View Saved Posts?
1. Click 💾 Saved Posts tab
2. View all engaged posts
3. Click ✖ Unsave to remove
4. Click 🔗 View to open Reddit
